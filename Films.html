The Tamil film Maharaja, featuring Vijay Sethupathi and Anurag Kashyap, has been living up to its name. Marking Sethupathi's 50th film, Maharaja was released on the streaming giant Netflix in June. While it was released in India on June 14, it was released within a week in other countries all over the globe, including Middle-Eastern, South Asian and East Asian nations.
