Raebareli: Rahul and Dinesh Singh were seen to be at loggerheads, they did not talk to each other, the resentment for commenting on sister Priyanka was visible

After the comment on sister Priyanka Gandhi, MP Rahul Gandhi and state minister Dinesh Pratap Singh came face to face for the first time on Tuesday. The eyes of both the leaders met, but their hearts did not meet. Neither was there any warmth between them during this time. During the meeting of Disha, the chairs of Rahul and Dinesh were next to each other. Rahul kept a serious posture. He did not talk to Dinesh directly. It was definitely that whatever questions were asked during the meeting, Rahul answered them. There was a time when the state's Minister of State Dinesh Pratap Singh was considered to be the closest to the Gandhi family. In 2019, Dinesh gave a jolt to the Congress by joining the BJP in the presence of Union Home Minister Amit Shah. After joining the BJP, Dinesh was given the responsibility of a minister. Since then, the distance between the Gandhi family and Dinesh has not been reduced till date. From time to time, Dinesh keeps attacking the Gandhi family. When Dinesh commented on Priyanka Gandhi for contesting from Wayanad in October, this conflict came to the fore again. There were a lot of protests from the Congressmen regarding this. When Rahul participated in the Disha meeting, everyone's eyes were on what he had talked to Dinesh. Will there be the same warmth as before.
